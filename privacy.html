Privacy Policy

CreatorDesk Publish helps creators prepare, review, and publish short-form video posts. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

We may collect account information you provide, such as your name, email address, creator handle, draft captions, upload metadata, and support messages. If you connect a TikTok account, we may receive information TikTok makes available through its approved APIs, such as profile identifiers, creator permission settings, upload status, and content publishing responses.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide the publishing workspace, prepare posts, show draft and upload status, maintain security, respond to support requests, and comply with platform and legal requirements. We do not sell personal information.

TikTok Data

TikTok data is used only to provide creator-authorized publishing and account-connected features. Publishing actions should require the creator's authorization and final confirmation. You can revoke TikTok access through your TikTok account settings or by contacting us.

Data Retention

We keep information only as long as needed to provide the service, meet legal requirements, resolve disputes, and maintain security. You can request deletion of your account data by contacting us.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure.
